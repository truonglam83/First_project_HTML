Stay in the heart of Ho Chi Minh City

One of our top picks in Ho Chi Minh City.

-Located 300 m from Tao Dan Park in District 1, Fusion Suites Saigon offers contemporary accommodations in the vibrant Ho Chi Minh City. The hotel operates a 24-hour front desk , a fitness center, and an on-site restaurant.

-Each room at Fusion Suites Saigon comes air-conditioned and is fitted with floor-to-ceiling windows, a flat-screen TV, as well as kitchenette. Private bathroom comes with either a bathtub or shower, slippers, bathrobes and free toiletries.

-Reunification Palace is 801 m from Fusion Suites Saigon, while War Remnants Museum is 801 m away. The nearest airport is Tan Son Nhat International Airport, 4.3 mi from Fusion Suites Saigon.

-Fresh, offering both indoor and outdoor seating areas, serves a range of delectable Vietnamese delights and international dishes.

-Couples in particular like the location – they rated it 9.0 for a two-person trip.
